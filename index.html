About me

I am a Mathematics PhD student at Université Paris Cite and Institut de Mathématiques de Jussieu-Paris Rive Gauche under the supervision of Sylvy Anscombe. My main interests lie in the intersection of Model Theory and Algebra, namely in the Model Theory of Valued Fields and their classification theoretical properties like NIP and NTP2.

You can find me at paulo.soto(at)imj-prg(dot)fr
